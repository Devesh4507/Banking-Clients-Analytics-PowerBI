# Banking Clients Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes banking client data using Power BI to evaluate customer financial behavior, credit exposure, and portfolio risk distribution.

The dashboard visualizes key financial metrics across 3000 banking clients including deposits, loans, income, and investment patterns.

---

## Tools Used

Power BI  
DAX Measures  
Interactive Visualizations  
Data Modeling  

---

## Dataset

Rows: 3000 clients

Key fields:

Age  
Nationality  
Occupation  
Bank Loans  
Bank Deposits  
Credit Card Balance  
Estimated Income  
Fee Structure  
Risk Weighting  

---

## Dashboard Features

KPI Cards
- Average Superannuation Savings
- Average Estimated Income
- Average Foreign Currency Accounts
- Average Overdraft Lending
- Total Bank Loans
- Total Bank Deposits

Charts
- Risk Weighting Distribution
- Credit Card Balance by Fee Structure
- Occupation Distribution by Nationality

Filters
- Nationality slicer

---

## Key Insights

Total Bank Deposits: $2.01B  
Total Bank Loans: $1.77B  

Average Client Income: $171K  

Highest Credit Card Balance segment:
High Fee Structure clients

Majority of clients fall into moderate risk weighting categories.
